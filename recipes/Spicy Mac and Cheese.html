Ghost Pepper Mac and Cheese

Instructions:

This is a recipe for a simple spicy mac and cheese recipe that is impossible to screw up.

    Ingredients
  • Cheese Club (Aldi) Mac and Cheese
  • Ghost Pepper Sauce
    Steps
  1. Boil the Cheese Club Mac and Cheese for 8-10 minutes into a medium size pot.
  2. Strain the water.
  3. Add the Mac and Cheese, cheese sauce.
  4. Add in the hottest sauce you have on hand, preferably ghost pepper sauce.
  5. Stir and enjoy!
