Chicken and Ramen

Instructions:

Don't let the picture fool you. This is going to be significantly worse. You probably should find a real cookbook.

    Ingredients
  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 0.5-1 lb of chicken tenderloins
    Steps
  1. Heat chicken in a nonpreheated air fryer at 390 degrees for 18 minutes.
  2. Bring Water to a boil in a medium size pot
  3. When the chicken has 5 minutes left: Boil the Ramen vigourously for about 2 minutes and 45 seconds in a medium size pot.
  4. Strain the water.
  5. Add the Ramen to a small bowl and mix in flavoring.
  6. Add in the hottest sauce you have on hand, preferably ghost pepper sauce.
  7. Add in chicken and enjoy!
